Services

The following are all the services I offer as a whole package. I also take on each of the tasks individually if needed.

Design

Bespoke websites built based on the customers needs. After an initial meeting, where I gauge your needs I set out to make your dream website. Researching modern trends, designing a logo, determining suitable colour palletes all goes into building your brand.

Develop

After the design has been signed off, I begin coding the website using industry best practices to ensure I deliver a quality product, using a variety of languages as the project requires to produce a modern responsive website. With frequent check-ins you'll always be in the loop.

Deploy

Once the design and build process is finalised the site is tested rigourously across a range of devices. Upon a pass of these tests, we move on to the final stage of publishing your website to the internet. This entails sourcing a domain for the website, if needed, and finding a suitable hosting solution.
